	Mono acting is act of a single actor in the stage. <br>
	Acting of a play in the stage consist more than one character and based on 'dialogues'  i.e conversation of characters.<br> 
	Mono acting is based on 'Monologues'. <br>
	All the ingredients of a stage play - light, costume, effect music etc can be can be used according to the theme of the play. <br>
	Only difference is in mono acting only a single character acts.
